# Specification Standard

## Scope
All project specifications within the AIEF framework MUST adhere to this standard.

## Structure
1. **Metadata Section** – YAML front‑matter containing title, authors, version, date, and tags.
2. **Executive Summary** – One paragraph summarizing purpose and scope.
3. **Context & Background** – Business context and constraints.
4. **Requirements** – Ordered list of functional and non‑functional requirements.
5. **Design Decisions** – Reference to corresponding ADRs.
6. **Milestones** – Sequence of deliverable milestones with dates.
7. **Acceptance Criteria** – Testable statements for each requirement.
8. **Dependencies & Risks** – External dependencies, risk matrix.
9. **Appendices** – Glossary, references, diagrams.

## Naming Convention
All specification files MUST be named `PROJECTNAME-SPEC-VERSION.md` and placed in the `specifications/` directory.

# Review Checklist

The following checklist MUST be used when reviewing a specification document:
1. **Metadata** – Verify presence and correctness of all required keys.
2. **Executive Summary** – Does it capture purpose, scope, and boundaries?
3. **Requirements** – Are they specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time‑bound (SMART)?
4. **Design Decisions** – Are corresponding ADRs referenced and linked?
5. **Milestones** – Are they realistic, prioritized, and aligned with dependencies?
6. **Acceptance Criteria** – Do they provide unambiguous testable conditions?
7. **Dependencies & Risks** – Is a risk assessment present and mitigation strategies described?
8. **Formatting & Style** – Does the document follow Markdown best practices and the specification standard structure?

Any deviation MUST be noted, corrected, or justified in the review comments.

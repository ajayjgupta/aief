# Engineering Principles of AIEF

1. **Clarity** – Code, tests, and documentation should be immediately understandable.
2. **Modularity** – Components must have a single responsibility and well‑defined interfaces.
3. **Test‑First Validation** – Every change is accompanied by automated tests covering all relevant edge cases.
4. **Security by Design** – Threat modeling, input validation, and least privilege are built into every layer.
5. **Continuous Improvement** – Retrospectives and metrics drive incremental enhancements.
6. **Human‑AI Co‑Creation** – AI augments human judgment; humans retain final decision authority.
